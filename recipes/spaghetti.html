Spaghetti

We ate this a lot growing up, as I'm sure most have! This is my favorite rendition personally and definitely worth the try.

Will feed a family of 2-3 for one evening. Good refrigerated for up to 2 days.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cubes beef bouillion
  • 8 oz can tomato sauce
  • 6 oz can tomato paste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups hot water
  • 2 tsp sugar - white or brown
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • Dash of garlic
  • 8 oz / 227g spaghetti noodles

Steps

  1. Cook and drain ground beef when it turns brown
  2. Throw in salt, pepper, tomato sauce, tomato paste, water (with bouillion cubes in it), sugar, basil, oregano, and garlic.
  3. Simmer on low for an hour
  4. A few minutes before the hour is passed, cook the spaghetti noodles as directed on the package
  5. Once noodles are cooked, drain and add to spaghetti sauce
